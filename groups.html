Lion Dance Team

About: LDT is a non-audition lion dance team. We are the only group on the central coast and we perform for event inside Cal Poly as well as bless restaurants, temples and weddings outside of Cal Poly. No experience required.

Take Out Kidz

About: Take Out Kidz is a non-audition dance group that performs at school and CSA events such as Culture Fest and Chinese New Year Banquet. The choreographies usually involve a mix of hip-hop, urban, contemporary, and jazz. We are a family for anyone who wishes to learn to dance, create new dance, and, most importantly, have fun!


Click here to check out TOK's past performances!

Practices: Wednesdays 6-8 PM in PAC parking lot, Fridays and Sundays 5-7 PM in Rec Center Studio 3
