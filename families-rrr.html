RRRGLF

In RRR, our motto “Work hard, Play hard” defines our family’s culture. We’re a welcoming and laid back family that bonds through struggling together as well as supporting each other through our endless midterms and finals seasons. We have family members from all different sorts of majors and concentrations who can act as a helpful resource and a strong support system for littles and other family members. Now of course, though academics may be one of our strong suits, that definitely isn't all that there is to our family ;) After a stressful week, we’re not afraid to go all out and have some fun and through the many events and outings we have planned for our members and littles we’re sure that our members will find a sense of belonging and gain a loving community to fall back on!
